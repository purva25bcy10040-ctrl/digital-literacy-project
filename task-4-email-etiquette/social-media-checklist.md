 Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students
 Do's
- Maintain respectful communication online.
- Share academic achievements professionally.
- Verify information before posting or sharing.
- Protect personal and private information.
- Use strong privacy settings on accounts.
 Don'ts
- Do not share offensive or harmful content.
- Avoid oversharing personal details.
- Do not engage in online arguments or harassment.
- Avoid posting misleading information.
- Do not click or share suspicious links.
